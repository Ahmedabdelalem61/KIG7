1. Logging in

  1. Open your browser and go to http://<your-server>:9073 (your IT will provide the exact address).
  2. Enter your e-mail and password, click Log in.
  3. From the apps screen, click the HR UAE tile (anchor icon).
Tip. Bookmark the page after first login. Use the magnifier icon at the top of any list to search instantly.

2. Roles & permissions

Your menu choices change with the role your administrator assigned to you.

RoleWhat you can do
UserSee own profile, request leaves, view own payslips.
OfficerManage employees, documents, leaves, flight tickets.
FinanceApprove flight tickets, run payroll, salary adjustments.
ManagerEverything above + terminations, dashboards, audit reports.
Don't see a menu? You may not have the role for it. Ask your administrator.

3. Screen layout (1 minute tour)

  • Top bar: the breadcrumb shows where you are. Use the search bar (right) to find anything.
  • Form vs list: a list shows many records, click any line to open the form view.
  • Edit: just start typing in any field; changes are saved when you click Save (cloud icon at the top-left) or move to another record.
  • Smart buttons (top-right of a form) jump to related records — e.g. Documents, Audit Trail, Payslips on an employee.
  • Action menu (gear icon ⚙) at the top of a list lets you print reports, archive, etc.

4. Adding a new employee Officer

Master Data Auto Cost Center Audit logged

HR UAE ▸ Master Data ▸ Employees ▸ New

  1. Type the Name exactly as shown in the passport (e.g. Rodriguez Garcia Pedro).
  2. Pick a Rank and Position from the drop-downs (these are pre-loaded for UAE).
  3. Fill Passport No., Roster (e.g. A08521), Date of Birth and Visa Expiry.
  4. Optionally choose a Project (analytic). The system will auto-create a Cost Center matching the employee.
  5. Click Save (cloud icon, top-left).
  6. Open the Contract tab to add salary, start date and assign the UAE Standard structure.
What you get automatically: Age is calculated from DOB, visa status (active / expiring / expired) is computed, an audit-trail entry is created, and a "cost center" analytic account is generated.
Bulk import (50+ employees at once)
  1. Open the Employees list.
  2. Click ⚙ Actions ▸ Import.
  3. Download the template, fill it in Excel, then upload.

5. Uploading typed documents Officer

Employee form ▸ Documents smart-button

  1. Open the employee, click the Documents smart button.
  2. Click New, choose a Type (Passport, Visa, Medical, License, Other).
  3. Set the Expiry date, then drag-and-drop the file in the Attachment field.
  4. Save.
Expiry alerts. A nightly job watches every document. 30 days before expiry the system e-mails the employee, the assigned officer and the line manager. The "Documents" smart button shows a red counter for documents expiring soon.

6. Requesting & approving a leave User · Officer

HR UAE ▸ Time Off ▸ My Leaves

  1. Click New. Choose a Leave Type (Annual, Special, Medical, Unpaid).
  2. Pick the From and To dates. The system blocks duplicates and checks balances.
  3. Click Confirm to submit.
  4. An officer opens the To Approve tab and clicks Validate.
What happens automatically when a leave is validated
  • The employee's Status field switches (e.g. on_annual_leave) — useful in the dashboard.
  • If a payslip exists for the leave period, it is automatically moved to On Hold and shows the held amount and the payable amount.
  • An e-mail goes out 5 days before the return date.
Mark a return: open the leave and tick Returned — the held payslip is released and goes back to Done.

7. Booking a flight ticket Officer · Finance

HR UAE ▸ Flights ▸ New

  1. Pick the Employee. The form auto-fills passport, DOJ and project.
  2. Select From / To Country, departure date, and the purpose (Hire, Annual Leave, Replacement, Termination).
  3. Enter the Ticket Price and any extras (luggage / fees). The total updates live.
  4. Choose the Agency and tick Refundable if applicable.
  5. Click Book. An expense entry is created and linked to the employee + project.

8. Salary adjustment, allowance or deduction Manager

HR UAE ▸ Payroll ▸ Salary Adjustments ▸ New

  1. Pick the Employee.
  2. Choose the Kind: Adjustment, Allowance or Deduction.
  3. Pick the Mode:
    • One-shot — applies to a specific payslip.
    • Date range — applies to every payslip whose period intersects the range.
    • Recurring — re-applied automatically every month by a nightly job.
  4. Enter the Amount in AED, add notes if needed.
  5. Click Submit → Approve (HR Manager). The payslip input line is pushed automatically.

9. Running monthly payroll Finance · Manager

HR UAE ▸ Payroll ▸ Payslips

  1. Click Payslip Batches ▸ New, choose a name (e.g. February 2026) and the month.
  2. Click Generate Payslips. Pick all employees or a filtered set.
  3. Open each payslip, hit Compute Sheet to recalculate.
  4. Review the Held / Payable values for employees on leave.
  5. When ready, click Confirm and then Make Payment.
Salary structure used: UAE Standard — Basic + Housing + Transport ± Adjustments − Unpaid leave − Flight deductions = Net. Currency is locked to AED.
How are payslips put on hold?

When a leave is validated and overlaps the payslip period, a hold is calculated using (wage / 30) × held days. The payable amount is the rest. When the employee is marked Returned, the hold is released and the payslip returns to Done.

10. Terminating an employee Manager

HR UAE ▸ Termination ▸ New

  1. Pick the Employee — the contract is auto-loaded.
  2. Choose Departure date (last day in UAE) and Arrival date in home country.
  3. Pick a Reason (resignation, end of contract, dismissal, …) and add notes.
  4. Click Activate. The employee is archived, the contract is set to Closed, and any future payslips are cancelled.
  5. When all paperwork is done, click Close to lock the record.
Activation cannot be undone. Use the Reset to Draft button on the same form before activating to roll back changes.

13. The audit trail

Every change to employees, contracts, documents, leaves, flights, payslips and terminations is recorded automatically.

Employee form ▸ Audit Trail smart-button

  • Each entry shows who changed what, when, and from / to values in human-readable form.
  • Use the Audit Report wizard (HR UAE ▸ Reports ▸ Audit Report) to print a per-employee history for any date range.
Audit entries cannot be edited or deleted by users — even managers — to keep the trail trustworthy.

15. Troubleshooting / FAQ

I can't see the HR UAE app. Ask your administrator to assign you a role from the HR UAE security group. After re-login, the app appears.
The "Documents" smart button shows a red number. That number is documents expiring within 30 days. Click it, renew them and update the expiry date.
Why is a payslip "On Hold"? Because an active leave overlaps the payslip period. Open the payslip — the held amount and reason are shown. Tick Returned on the leave to release.
I created an employee with the wrong name. Now what? Open the employee form, edit the field, click Save. Both the employee and the auto-cost-center are renamed and an audit-trail entry is created.
How do I undo a termination? Open the termination, click Reset to Draft. The employee is unarchived and the contract reopened. Once you click Close, this is no longer possible.
I can't add a salary adjustment. Salary adjustments require the HR Manager (UAE) role to approve. Submit it and ask a manager to approve.
